Microphone not working?

  • 1. Refresh the page: Sometimes the browser just needs a fresh start.
  • 2. Click "Allow": Look at the top left of your browser. If you see a popup, click Allow.
  • 3. Check the address bar: Click the Lock icon (🔒) or Settings icon next to the URL and make sure "Microphone" is turned ON.
  • 4. Use Chrome/Edge: This app works best in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.

Tip: Stay in a quiet environment for better accuracy.
